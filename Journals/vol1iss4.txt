<H2>
This issue explore the'sign' 'nature' by which 
healthy beings leave mark of their identity. 
The issue focuses particularly on the immune system, and the 
immunological homunculus, as presented in two major papers of 
Irun Cohen and Graham Jones. Herbal studies provide further 
evidence of relevant signatures of plants in their composition 
and their healing aptitude.
