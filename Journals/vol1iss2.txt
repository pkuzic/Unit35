Introduction

<H2>
Turning Leaves is an autumn issue at the turning point of the year, 
looking underneath the current paradigm at new shoots that are coming up. 
The articles reveal underneath the old dying leaves, something novel that is arising. 
Goethean science, Inclusionality, Complexity, Leonardo's science, Quantum Theory, Process 
are all to be found in the pages, in between the tale of the Ox herder seeking 
wholeness in the analogy of the lost Ox that disappears and reappears. A stepping stone 
leads into the adult wisdom of the journal. 
</H2>
